About GreenyGrocery

Connecting local farmers with conscious consumers since 2023

Our Story

GreenyGrocery started with a simple idea: to make fresh, locally grown produce accessible to everyone while supporting sustainable farming practices and local communities.

Founded in 2023 by a group of passionate food lovers and environmental advocates, we recognized the disconnect between consumers and the source of their food. We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap by creating a direct farm-to-table service that benefits both consumers and local farmers.

What began as a small operation serving just 50 families in our local neighborhood has now grown into a thriving community-supported business delivering thousands of boxes of fresh produce weekly across the region.

Despite our growth, our mission remains the same: to provide the freshest, most nutritious produce while promoting sustainable agriculture and supporting local farmers.

Our Mission & Values

Sustainability
